FAQ

Q: Where does all of your material come from?

A: Most of our natural materials we find ourselves on the coast of New Jersey or in our travels! Other accent pieces, such as beads and trinkets come from thrift stores and fleamarkets.


Q: Can I hang my piece outside?

A: We don't recommend it. Dealing with the elements will shorten the lifespan of your piece. Treat it as if you would any other piece of art.

A: Right now, TBD. But you can follow us on Instagram to keep up with all the latest BPC news!
